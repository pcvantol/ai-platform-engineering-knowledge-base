## Certified Statement

Assurance review should require durable evidence, traceability, readiness status, decisions and pending gates before engineering acceptance.

## Engineering Rationale

Assurance decisions remain auditable when review reports expose the evidence used, decisions taken, unresolved gates and readiness state instead of relying on transient discussion.
