# Knowledge Concepts

This area contains knowledge concepts.

A knowledge concept is a refined knowledge candidate expressed as an explicit engineering concept. A concept is not yet certified and is not the highest authoritative form of knowledge.

Concepts prepare knowledge for generalization by making assumptions, boundaries, terminology, and engineering relevance explicit.

The operational concept formation command is `aikb review`, documented in [../cli/README.md](../cli/README.md). It transforms Knowledge Candidates into Knowledge Concepts only.
