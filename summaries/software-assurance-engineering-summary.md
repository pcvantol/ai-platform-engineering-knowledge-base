# Software Assurance Engineering Summary

## Scope

This summary describes the Generation 2 Software Assurance Engineering population of the Knowledge Base from DJConnect evidence.

## Summary

DJConnect Software Assurance evidence now provides reusable knowledge about:

- Software Assurance governance as a discipline separate from behavioral verification;
- engineering confidence based on multiple quality themes;
- evidence governance and evidence provenance;
- baseline-gated assurance readiness;
- residual risk visibility;
- assurance review and reporting traceability;
- durable evidence requirements before engineering acceptance.

## Evidence Sources

The corpus draws from:

- Software Assurance architecture documents;
- Software Assurance implementation readiness documents;
- phase completion protocol evidence;
- management summaries;
- qualification reports;
- coverage and baseline evidence.

## Certification Boundary

Only generalized knowledge with explicit evidence and complete lineage was certified. Unimplemented tooling, unevidenced operational controls, accepted residual risk decisions and product-specific quality claims were not certified.
