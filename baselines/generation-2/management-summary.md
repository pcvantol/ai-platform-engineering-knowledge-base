# Management Summary

Generation 2 of the AI Platform Engineering Knowledge Base is complete.

DJConnect has become the first canonical engineering corpus. The corpus covers Platform Engineering, Verification Engineering, Software Assurance Engineering, Release Engineering and Knowledge Engineering.

## Management Decision

Generation 2 may be baselined as:

```text
AI_PLATFORM_ENGINEERING_KNOWLEDGE_CORPUS_BASELINE_1
```

## Management Implications

- Generation 2 is frozen as a reviewed corpus baseline.
- Future work extends the corpus rather than rewriting it.
- Certified Knowledge remains authoritative.
- Publications may be generated later from Certified Knowledge only.
- New evidence must enter through Knowledge Sources and the governed lifecycle.
- Continuous evolution may revise, supersede or extend knowledge without erasing history.
