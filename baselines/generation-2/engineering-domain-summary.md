## Cross-Domain Relationships

```text
Platform Engineering
->
Verification Engineering
->
Software Assurance Engineering
->
Release Engineering
->
Knowledge Engineering
```

Relationship interpretation:

- Platform Engineering provides ownership and runtime boundaries.
- Verification Engineering produces evidence and qualification signals.
- Software Assurance Engineering governs confidence, readiness and risk using evidence.
- Release Engineering packages baselines, release identity, readiness and closure.
- Knowledge Engineering preserves evidence, lifecycle state, certification, publication readiness and future evolution.
