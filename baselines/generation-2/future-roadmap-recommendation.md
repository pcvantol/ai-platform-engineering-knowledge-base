# Future Roadmap Recommendation

Generation 2 is complete. Future work should proceed through continuous knowledge evolution.

## Recommended Direction

Future roadmap work should prioritize:

- onboarding additional Knowledge Sources;
- extracting additional evidence;
- expanding engineering domains;
- evolving Certified Knowledge through governed revisions;
- generating publications from Certified Knowledge;
- adding relationship and retrieval improvements;
- running periodic corpus qualification reviews.

## Avoided Direction

Future work should avoid:

- rewriting Generation 2 history;
- bypassing lifecycle traceability;
- generating publications from non-certified knowledge;
- treating DJConnect as canonical authority;
- merging or deleting Certified Knowledge without governed evolution.

## Roadmap Decision

The next phase should extend the corpus rather than rebuild it.
